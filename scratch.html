Golf

How I approach a round

Golf is the main way I reset during a busy semester. This page walks through how I think about a round, from warm‑up to the last putt.

Pre‑round routine

  1. Get ready at the range
    • Start with short wedges to loosen up and find contact.
    • Work up through the bag, finishing with the club used on the first tee.
  2. Walk the course in your head
    • Pick targets that keep big numbers off the card.
    • Decide where “smart bogey” is better than forced hero shots.
  3. Commit to every shot
    • Choose a clear target and shot shape before stepping in.
    • Accept whatever happens and move on to the next swing.
